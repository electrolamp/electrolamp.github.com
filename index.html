Electricity
the science, engineering, technology and physical phenomena associated with the presence and flow of electric charges. Electricity gives a wide variety of well-known electrical effects, such as lightning, static electricity, electromagnetic induction and the flow of electrical current in an electrical wire.

All matter is made up of atoms. Atoms have electrons, protons and neutrons. So everything you see around you has electrons and has electrical properties.
